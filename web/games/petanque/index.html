Pétanque

Throw your boules closest to the little jack — on a rough gravel piste that never plays quite fair.

Pitchstraight
Set a shot in three steps, then fire it. 1 · Line & power: drag out from the throwing circle — the way you drag is your line, how far you drag is the power (the ring shows angle and power, and a dotted arc previews the flight). It's force, not a target, so aiming straight at the jack and yanking hard sails long past it. 2 · Spin: on the little steel ball, drag the dot up to roll on, down to lob and drop dead, or out to hook left/right. 3 · Launch ▶ (bottom-right) to throw. The trajectory stays put while you tweak it, so nothing fires until you say so. Closest boule to the jack wins the end; first to 13 wins. A boule off the piste is dead. 2–4 players (pick a mode top-right, incl. AI self-play to watch) — each has a colour and a shape (● ◎ ■ ▲) so boules are easy to tell apart.
