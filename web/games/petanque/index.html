Pétanque

Throw your boules closest to the little jack — on a rough gravel piste that never plays quite fair.

You 0
0 Comp
Pitchstraight
Set your spin first on the little steel ball, snooker-style — drag the dot up to roll on, down to lob and drop dead, or out to the side to hook the flight left/right (double-click to centre). Then drag out from the throwing circle to throw: the way you drag is your line, and how far you drag is the power — the ring shows angle and power, and a dotted arc previews the flight. It's force, not a target, so aiming straight at the jack and yanking hard sails long past it. Closest boule to the jack wins the end; first to 13 wins. A boule off the piste is dead.
